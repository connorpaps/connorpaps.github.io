Getting Started - How to Use Collection Manager

Follow the steps below to create your first collection using Collection Manager and begin to explore all the features
that Collection Manager has to offer. Visit theKnowledge Hubfor more help.


Step 1) Create a Storage Box.

Storage Boxes help identify where your belongings are being kept and are used within your collections to show where those items are located.


Step 2) Create a Collection.

Collections are where you add your belongings to be stored. Create different collections to group different items together for easy searching and sorting.


Step 3) Add Items to Your Collection.

Within each of your collections you can add items which represent your belongings and link them to a storage box so you always know where they are stored.
